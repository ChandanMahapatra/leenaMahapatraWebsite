About

Leena is a creative genius from Orissa, India. Trained as an art and craft teacher, over the last two decades she has created a variety of art while giving it a local taste; e.g. Tanjore paintings, Madhubani paintings and coffee paintings. While she excels at painting landscapes, she can also make wonderful spiritual paintings (mostly of Indian gods and goddesses).
